✦ Going to Niagara Falls?

Absolutely worth the day trip. Five ways to get there:

💡Want it effortless? Browse our Niagara day-trip tours — transport included, with winery stops and optional boat cruises.
🚆 GO Transit — ~2–2.5 h

GO train from Union Station. When the direct Niagara train isn't running, take the Lakeshore West line to Burlington and transfer to GO Bus 12 — it drops you across from the Niagara Falls GO station. There's a GO + WEGO combo pass that includes local Niagara transit.

From Union Station Bus Terminal. Often the fastest and cheapest — details here.
