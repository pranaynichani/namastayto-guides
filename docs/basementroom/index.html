🚽 How to use the standalone bidet

Your bathroom has a European-style standalone bidet — the separate fixture beside the toilet.

  1. Adjust the hot/cold knobs to a comfortable temperature before sitting
  2. Sit facing the wall and clean yourself
  3. Turn it off and towel dry
🚫Nothing goes down the bidet — no toilet paper, nothing. It will clog. And it's for cleaning only — please don't pee in it 😅
🚪 Shower doors — gentle please

Only the outer door (with the handle) slides. The inner door isn't supposed to move — a gentle slide is all it takes.

⚠️Toilet paper only, please. No hair, wipes, tampons, paper towels, or kleenex down the toilet.
💧This is a dry bathroom — please keep water inside the tub; there's no floor drain outside the shower.

✦ Help & FAQ

🤝 Need anything, anytime

Fastest: message us on Airbnb. Or call Pranay 647-707-9594 / Emilee 416-573-5624 · WhatsApp · namastaytoronto@gmail.com

🆘In any emergency, call 911 first. Your exit is your suite entrance door; a second exit runs through the upstairs kitchen to the front or back of the house. Smoke & CO detectors are on every level, and an exit map is posted in your suite.
Can I check in early?

Check-in is 4 PM. If the suite is free earlier we're happy to accommodate — and we can arrange luggage drop-off either way. Just message us in advance.

Can I check out late?

Checkout is 11 AM. Late checkout isn't always possible, but ask — we'll do our best!

Is parking available?

Yes — city permits from $21.46/24h (we can print yours!), and you'll usually find a spot right on Concord Ave. There's also a Green P lot at 745 Ossington Ave. See Getting here.

Can I use the full kitchen?

Yes! The full shared kitchen is upstairs — keep your food in your basement fridge, and always run the vent fan when cooking (it's a gas stove). Your suite has its own plates, bowls, mugs, glasses and cutlery — please use those where you can, and if you do borrow anything from upstairs, return it upstairs. And most importantly, please clean up after yourself 💛

Is laundry available?

Yes! First 3 loads a week free, then $2.50 per load for washer and dryer. Detergent, softener and dryer sheets provided — and no one else needs the laundry room while you're here.

Is the suite allergy-friendly?

Kumbah 🐶 and Oliver 🐱 live upstairs and never enter your suite, but with central HVAC we can't guarantee an allergy-free environment.

Are there bugs?

Basements being basements, you might meet the occasional spider or ant, especially in wet weather. We treat preventatively and keep bug spray under the bathroom sink. Keeping windows and doors closed helps — and we apologize in advance for any six-legged visitors!
