✦ Help & FAQ

🤝 Need anything, anytime

Fastest: message us on Airbnb. Or call / WhatsApp Pranay at 647-707-9594 · email namastaytoronto@gmail.com

🆘In any emergency, call 911 first. Your exit is the front door; a second exit runs through the kitchen to the backyard. Smoke & CO detectors are on every level, and an exit map is posted in your room.
Can I check in early?

Check-in is 4 PM. If the room is free earlier we're happy to accommodate — and we can arrange luggage drop-off either way. Just message us in advance.

Can I check out late?

Checkout is 11 AM. Late checkout isn't always possible, but ask — we'll do our best!

Is parking available?

Yes — city permits from $21.46/24h (we can print yours!), and you'll usually find a spot right on Concord Ave. There's also a Green P lot at 745 Ossington Ave. See Getting here.

Can I use the kitchen?

Yes! The ground-floor kitchen is shared by all guests. Keep your food in the 2nd-floor fridge, and always run the vent fan when cooking.

Is laundry available?

Yes, with a minimum 5-night stay — first 3 loads a week free, then $2.50 per load for washer and dryer. Detergent and softener provided; let us know when you'd like to run a load.

Are there pets in the home?

Yes — Kumbah the dog 🐶 and Oliver the cat 🐱. They never enter your room or the bathroom, though you might hear a bark now and then. We can't guarantee an allergy-free environment due to the shared HVAC.
